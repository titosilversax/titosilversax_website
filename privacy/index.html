Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026  ·  titosilversax.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit titosilversax.com or purchase a digital product, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Who We Are

This site is operated by Alberto Martir, professionally known as Tito Silversax, based in Cleveland, Ohio, USA. You can reach us at hello@titosilversax.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: When you make a purchase, you provide your name, email address, and payment details through our checkout provider, Kit (formerly ConvertKit). We receive your name and email; your payment card details are handled directly by Kit's payment processor and are never stored by us.

Information collected automatically: When you visit this site, certain data is collected automatically through third-party tools (described below), including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and general location. This data is used for analytics and advertising measurement purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your purchased digital product via email
  • To send order confirmations and download links
  • To respond to support inquiries
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising (via Meta Pixel)
  • To analyze site traffic and visitor behavior (via Google Tag Manager)
  • To send occasional emails about new products or updates, if you opt in

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Kit (ConvertKit): We use Kit to process payments and deliver digital products. Your name and email address are stored in Kit's platform. Kit's privacy policy is available at kit.com/privacy.

Meta Pixel (Facebook): We use the Meta Pixel to track page views and purchase events for advertising measurement on Facebook and Instagram. This may collect your IP address, browser information, and actions taken on this site. You can learn more at facebook.com/privacy/policy. To opt out of Meta's ad tracking, visit facebook.com/adpreferences.

Google Tag Manager: We use Google Tag Manager to manage analytics and tracking scripts on this site. Google Tag Manager itself does not collect personal data, but the tags it manages may. You can learn more at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies placed by the third-party services described above (Meta Pixel, Google Tag Manager). These are used to measure advertising performance and site traffic. By using this site, you consent to the use of these cookies.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of certain features.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and purchase records in Kit for as long as necessary to deliver your product, provide support, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe from any marketing emails via the link in any email we send
  • California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of your information (we do not sell personal information)
  • EEA/UK residents (GDPR): You have additional rights including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@titosilversax.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy should be directed to:
hello@titosilversax.com
